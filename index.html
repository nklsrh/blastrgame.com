Platforms PC/Mac/Linux + ?
Genre Third-person puzzle action
In development since July 2014

All the competitive antics of modern games without the gratuitous violence

Intended for consoles, BLASTR is a third-person ‘shooter’ built from the ground up to be enjoyed by anyone,
whether it be your best friend’s baby niece or your Bee Gees - loving granddad.



A world built of robots, by robots, and for robots

Exchange favours with a door to open access to other areas,
threaten platforms in order to make your way above a deep chasm,
and plead with panels to provide cover against suspecting Sentries.




Physics-altering high-tech gadgetry

Utilise the signature Blast to propel enemies away, or the Magnet to draw obstacles away from your path.



Work in Progress
